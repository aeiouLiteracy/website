A.E.I.O.U.: Technology, Mobility, Literacy, Phonics

Who is A.E.I.O.U.?

We are a group of educators working together for the purposes of creating a foundation of literacy for daycare centers and school age programs. We help children increase their basic literacy skills from the beginning of their preschool or daycare experience into Kindergarten and beyond. We are educators dedicated to helping children with basic literacy and computer skills.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to work with children in low income areas to teach them how to use educational technology devices i.e. tablets and laptop computers in order to bridge the digital divide. We increase early literacy skills by using phonemic awareness, phonics, vocabulary, comprehension and fluency in order to close the lireacy gap.
