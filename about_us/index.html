A.E.I.O.U. Technology, Mobility, Literacy, and Phonics

Who is A.E.I.O.U.?

We are a group of educators working together for the purposes of creating a foundation of literacy for daycare centers and school age programs. We help children increase their basic literacy skills from the beginning of their preschool or daycare experience into Kindergarten and beyond. We are educators dedicated to helping children with basic literacy and computer skills.

Purpose

  • To service students in low income areas
  • To provide children with computer literacy skills, beginning phonic skills, and early reading strategies
  • To familiarize children with laptop and tablet interaction
  • To help close the literacy gap and shrink the digital divide
