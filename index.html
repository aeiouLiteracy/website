Putting Daycare Centers and Early Childhood Development Programs First with Technology and Literacy

Mission Statement

Our mission is to work with children in a variety of economic based communites to teach them how to use educational technology devices i.e. tablets and laptop computers in order to bridge the digital divide. We increase early literacy skills by using phonemic awareness, phonics, vocabulary, comprehension and fluency in order to close the literacy gap.

Educational Philosophy

The A.E.I.O.U. educational philosophy begins with helping children to realize their creativity, self awareness and motivation to become inspired, organized, and structured individuals. We believe if children are shown they have talents and are allowed the right to express them, children will have an increased skill set that will be useful in the future. These skills such as accessory knowledge (i.e. tablets, laptops, and touchscreens devices), in addition to the basic components of literacy.

Children should be motivated to explore, dream and be challenged. They should also be taught to realize their dreams. The goal of A.E.I.O.U. is to help children find themselves and discover new things about who they are while promoting literacy skills.
