A.E.I.O.U. Services

A.E.I.O.U. is an educational organization that services on multiple levels.

  • Daycare Centers
  • Preschools / Head Start Programs
  • Pre - Kindergarten & Kindergarten

A.E.I.O.U services all types of early learning programs in and around the Chicagoland area We provide our services through local educational centers.

A.E.I.O.U. provides services to students through educational programs increasing computer literacy and phonics through its early literacy cirriculum

Little Children Learning to Love Literacy

Little Children Learning to Love Literacy is an educational program in assocation with A.E.I.O.U. Its focus is to educate children in the grades of Daycare, Preschool, Pre-Kindergarten, Kindergarten, and first grade. The purpose of this program is to introduce children to computers i.e. keyboards, mouse, and monitors. Little Children Learning to Love Litracy mainly works with Daycare Centers and School-aged programs. This educational program will work with children in the five component areas, which are alphabetic code, high frequency words, fluency, vocabulary and comprehension. Little Children Learning to Love Literacy will work with the children on an individual basis, and in a whole group and in a small group setting. This program will work with children on phonics, reading strategies, and sentence structure. Little Children Learning to Love Literacy will give children an opportunity to get familiar with interacting with computer devices at an early stage in their educational careers. Finally, to bridge the digital divide and help close the literacy gap.

On Site Program

A.E.I.O.U. provides a one month long onsite literacy program for daycare centers and school age programs. Through the Little Children Learning To Love Literacy program, two sites can be serviced simultaneously reaching children in twenty-four daycare sites per year.
